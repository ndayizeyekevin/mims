FAQ

The FAQ section provides answers to the most commonly asked questions about our Medication Insurance Management System (MIMS). Whether you're a pharmacist, insurance representative, or admin, this section will help you understand how to navigate the system, manage prescriptions, process insurance claims, and ensure data security. Explore the FAQs to get quick insights into the key features and functionality of MIMS.

read more Below
What is MIMS?

Answer: MIMS stands for Medication Insurance Management System, designed to help manage prescriptions, medications, insurance claims, and transactions across pharmacies, patients, and insurance companies. 

Who can use MIMS?

Answer: 

MIMS is designed for pharmacists, insurance companies, and administrators to manage medical prescription details, process insurance claims, and track prescriptions and transactions.

How does MIMS handle insurance claims?

Answer:

MIMS allows users to submit, approve, or reject insurance claims, tracking their status (pending, approved, rejected) and providing reasons for rejections when necessary. 

Can MIMS track prescription history?

Answer:

Yes, MIMS records all issued and filled prescriptions, linking them to patients, pharmacies, and medications. You can also attach digital copies of prescriptions for easy reference. 

How secure is my data in MIMS?

Answer:

MIMS ensures data security through role-based access control. Each user, such as pharmacists, admins, or insurance company representatives, is given access based on their role to ensure the privacy and security of the system.
